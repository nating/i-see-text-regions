    Develop a program to determine how many spoons are in a baby food can.  You are provided with three sample images and 18 test images.

    For the system you must compute the Precision, Recall, Accuracy, Specificity and F1 measure.  You can learn about these measures in Section 9.6.3 of the text accompanying this course.
