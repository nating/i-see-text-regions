• Locate printed notices

    Develop a program (in C++ using OpenCV) to locate printed text on notices (e.g. the image below shows a number of text areas with the ideal answer shown in red).  The text areas should be identified within rectangular regions with different regions for separated text and text of different sizes.

    Your code must also automatically evaluate the performance of your system in terms of the DICE coefficient per image and (separately) per ground truth region.  You must also summarise the overall performance of your solution over all eight test images and over all twenty ground truth regions, and discuss what the performance numbers show/mean.
